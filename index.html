Key Highlights
Momentum
All traffic metrics up 40%+ month-over-month. The site is growing consistently and keeping users more engaged.
Search Presence
Ranks position 3.4 for brand name searches, with a 53% CTR — well above the ~3–5% industry average.
First Order
The store completed its first live order ($406.25), confirming the full purchase flow is working end-to-end.
Opportunity
98 add-to-cart events with only 1 completed order. Recovering even 5% of abandoned carts could mean ~5 more orders in April.

Recommended Focus for April

  1. Cart abandonment recovery — An email sequence or retargeting campaign targeting the 98-to-1 drop-off is the single biggest revenue lever right now.
  2. Non-branded organic content — Nearly all search traffic today comes from people who already know the brand. Ranking for discovery keywords brings in new audiences.
  3. Spanish-language audience — The /es page has a 76% engagement rate, the highest on the site, suggesting an underserved, high-intent audience.
